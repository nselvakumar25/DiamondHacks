Name: Pirate Polls
Authors: Stella Kim, Adrianne Nguyen, Nishitha Selvakumar, Vanessa Tello
Description: We need government action to become more accessible to everyone. This resource allows you to stay informed in government figures, policies, and voting days to cast your choice in your representatives. It includes information on how our government, voter registration, when and where to vote, and current laws and propositions that we can vote on.
